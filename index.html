WHALES IN THE MIRISSA SEA

Mirissa, on Sri Lanka’s south coast, is a top spot for whale watching, with blue whales, sperm whales, and orcas frequenting its waters from November to April.
HUMPBACK WHALE
Humpback whales live up to 50 years, reaching 12–16 meters in length and weighing around 25–30 tons.
See More About
BLUE WHALE
Blue whales live up to 80–90 years, reaching lengths of 24–30 meters and weighing 100–150 tons, making them the largest animals on Earth.
See More About
WHALE SHARK
Whale sharks can live 70–100 years, reaching lengths of 12–18 meters and weighing around 20 tons, making them the largest fish species.
See More About
SPERM WHALE
Sperm whales live around 60–70 years, reaching lengths of 16–20 meters and weighing up to 45 tons, with males larger than females.
See More About
MINK WHALE
Minke whales live around 30–50 years, reaching lengths of 7–10 meters and weighing up to 10 tons, making them one of the smallest baleen whales.

Night Diving

Explore the Mysteries of the Ocean at Night! Join Mirissa Snorkeling Adventures for a unique experience diving into the ocean's depths under the cover of darkness.

Minimum Two Persons Required.

What We Offer:

  • Free Pickup and Drop-off
  • High-Quality Camera Provided

Our night dives start at 7:00 PM local time, allowing you to experience the underwater world after sunset.

Licensed Divers: If you have a valid diving license, you can explore depths of up to 15m/20m, allowing you to see more of the vibrant nocturnal marine life.

Non-Licensed Divers: If you do not have a diving license, you can still enjoy night diving up to a safe depth of 3m. Our guides will ensure you have a safe and enjoyable experience.

We offer discounted rates for licensed divers, making it an even more attractive adventure for experienced divers.

We provide all necessary diving gear, we guarantee your safety and comfort.
