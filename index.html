About Us

Diving Mirissa & Snorkeling Tours

Welcome to Diving Mirissa & Snorkeling Tours, where the thrill of the ocean meets unforgettable memories.

We specialize in creating extraordinary marine experiences for tourists looking to explore the wonders of the sea.

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Mirissa sea and swim alongside graceful turtles and majestic whales.

Whether you’re snorkeling, scuba diving, or joining our exciting fishing tours, each adventure promises a unique glimpse into the vibrant marine life of our beautiful coast.

At Diving Mirissa & Snorkeling Tours, we are passionate about sharing our love for the ocean with visitors from around the world. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing safe, sustainable, and enjoyable experiences for everyone. Join us and discover the breathtaking beauty of the ocean like never before!

Tour Snaps

Relive the magic of your ocean adventure with our Tour Snaps! We capture the best moments of each tour, from encounters with vibrant marine life to thrilling whale and turtle sightings. Take home these memories and cherish your time with us in Mirissa forever!
HUMPBACK WHALE
Humpback whales live up to 50 years, reaching 12–16 meters in length and weighing around 25–30 tons.
BLUE WHALE
Blue whales live up to 80–90 years, reaching lengths of 24–30 meters and weighing 100–150 tons, making them the largest animals on Earth.
WHALE SHARK
Whale sharks can live 70–100 years, reaching lengths of 12–18 meters and weighing around 20 tons, making them the largest fish species.
SPERM WHALE
Sperm whales live around 60–70 years, reaching lengths of 16–20 meters and weighing up to 45 tons, with males larger than females.
MINK WHALE
Minke whales live around 30–50 years, reaching lengths of 7–10 meters and weighing up to 10 tons, making them one of the smallest baleen whales.
